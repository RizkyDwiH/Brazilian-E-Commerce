# Brazilian E-Commerce Customer Analytics 
---

## Detailed Analysis & Insights

### 1. Customer Retention Crisis
**Problem Identified:**
- **94.24%** of customers make only one purchase
- Massive drop-off: **94.24% → 2.68% → 0.19%** across orders
- Only **5.76%** of customers return for repeat purchases

**Business Impact:**
- High customer acquisition costs
- Lost revenue potential from repeat business
- Opportunity: Convert one-time buyers to loyal customers

**Recommended Strategy:**
- Implement loyalty program with points system
- Launch "Second Purchase" discount campaign
- Personalized email marketing for inactive customers

### 2. Geographic Revenue Concentration
**Problem Identified:**
- **São Paulo dominates** with **$509M (37%)** of total revenue
- Top 3 states (SP, RJ, MG) account for **62%** of revenue
- High dependency on single geographic market

**Business Impact:**
- Vulnerability to regional economic changes
- Untapped potential in other states
- Opportunity: Geographic expansion

**Recommended Strategy:**
- Targeted marketing in Rio de Janeiro and Minas Gerais
- Optimize shipping logistics for non-SP states
- Regional promotional campaigns

### 3. Product Category Performance
**Problem Identified:**
- **Beauty & Health** leads with **$124M** revenue
- **Watches & Gifts** follows with **$117M**
- Significant gap between top and other categories

**Business Impact:**
- Revenue concentration in few categories
- Opportunity: Category diversification
- Strong foundation in high-performing categories

**Recommended Strategy:**
- Expand inventory in top-performing categories
- Cross-selling initiatives between categories
- Marketing focus on Beauty & Health leadership

### 4. Satisfaction-Retention Paradox
**Problem Identified:**
- High customer satisfaction (**4.15/5** for one-time, **4.21/5** for repeat)
- But extremely low retention rates
- Indicates non-product related issues

**Business Impact:**
- Customer experience issues (shipping, service, pricing)
- Marketing and engagement gaps
- Opportunity: Customer experience improvements

**Recommended Strategy:**
- Review shipping and delivery processes
- Implement customer feedback system
- Enhance post-purchase engagement
